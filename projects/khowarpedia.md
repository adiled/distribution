## What it is

A search-friendly Khowar to English dictionary using fuzzy search. Adaptable to any language dataset with semantics. JavaScript front-end.

Khowar (کھوار) is a Dardic language of Chitral and adjoining regions in northern Pakistan, spoken by 300k to 500k people. Endangered-language-adjacent. Written tradition is thin, dictionary resources online are sparse.

## Why this dossier exists

Khowarpedia is **deliberately out of the agentic-systems thesis** that drives the rest of the portfolio. It is a cultural project (language preservation tooling) and its distribution lives in a different orbit: linguistics communities, language-preservation initiatives, Khowar-speaking diaspora.

This dossier exists to record three things:
1. The project is *not forgotten*. It is in T5 by intent, not by neglect.
2. If a strategic moment arises (a research collaboration, a grant opportunity, a rebrand), the project is ready to be reactivated.
3. The agent-systems portfolio's distribution work should not contaminate the dictionary's audience by trying to push it through agentic-systems channels.
