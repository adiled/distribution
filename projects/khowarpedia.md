## What it is

A search-friendly Khowar–English dictionary using fuzzy search. Adaptable to any language dataset with semantics. JavaScript front-end.

Khowar (کھوار) is a Dardic language of Chitral and adjoining regions in northern Pakistan, spoken by ~300k–500k people. Endangered-language-adjacent: written tradition is thin, dictionary resources online are sparse.

## Why this dossier exists

Khowarpedia is **deliberately out of the agentic-systems thesis** that drives the rest of the portfolio. It is a cultural project — language preservation tooling — and its distribution lives in a different orbit: linguistics communities, language-preservation initiatives, Khowar-speaking diaspora.
